# Dashboard KPI Dictionary

This document explains the KPIs used in the taxi dashboard.

## Summary KPIs

| KPI | Meaning | How It Is Used |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Total Trips | Total cleaned trips in the modeled analytics layer | Understand project scale and demand volume |
| Total Revenue | Total modeled pickup revenue | Understand the financial size of the analyzed period |
| Manhattan Trip Share | Share of modeled pickup trips that start in Manhattan | Show how concentrated demand is geographically |
| Busiest Pickup Hour | Hour with the highest total pickups | Support shift planning and fleet availability |
| Route Alerts | Count of high-volume low-efficiency routes | Identify routes that need operational review |
| Zones Monitored | Number of TLC taxi zones in the dashboard | Show coverage of the geographic model |

## Fleet Allocation KPIs

| KPI | Meaning | How It Is Used |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Fleet Allocation Priority Score | Composite score based on demand, utilization, efficiency, and pressure | Rank zones where vehicle availability may need attention |
| Zone Utilization Proxy | Relative pickup activity compared with available observed hours | Identify zones with consistently high usage |
| Capacity Pressure Proxy | Pressure indicator based on peak demand compared with average demand | Flag zones that may become stressed during peaks |
| Operational Imbalance Score | Difference between pickups and dropoffs relative to total activity | Identify zones where supply and demand may be unbalanced |

## Demand KPIs

| KPI | Meaning | How It Is Used |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Trips Per Hour | Number of pickups in a zone during a specific hour | Identify when and where demand is strongest |
| Peak Hour Demand Index | Hourly demand compared with typical demand | Detect demand spikes |
| Demand Concentration by Zone | Share of system demand represented by a zone | Identify zones that dominate the market |
| Demand Volatility Score | Variability of hourly demand | Identify zones that may need flexible fleet planning |

## Revenue And Efficiency KPIs

| KPI | Meaning | How It Is Used |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Revenue Per Trip | Average revenue generated by each trip | Compare high-yield and low-yield areas |
| Revenue Per Mile | Revenue generated per mile traveled | Understand distance efficiency |
| Revenue Per Minute | Revenue generated per trip minute | Understand time efficiency |
| Congestion Proxy | Time required per mile | Flag areas where trips may be slowed by congestion |
| Revenue Concentration Risk | Degree to which revenue depends on a small number of areas | Identify overreliance on specific zones |

## Route KPIs

| KPI | Meaning | How It Is Used |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Route Productivity Index | Route revenue and volume productivity indicator | Compare route value |
| Route Efficiency Score | Combined route efficiency measure | Rank routes by operational quality |
| High-Volume Low-Efficiency Alert | Flag for routes with high trip volume but weak efficiency | Prioritize route review |

## Why I Made This File

```text
I created a KPI dictionary so the dashboard metrics are defined in one place. This makes the analysis easier to explain and reduces confusion when the same metric appears in more than one chart.
```
